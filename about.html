Purpose and Vision

Our carbon footprint calculator helps individuals and organizations understand and manage their environmental impact. We aim to provide a tool that not only measures emissions but also offers actionable insights and strategies for reduction.

Key Features

Comprehensive Tracking

Track transport, home energy, food, waste and purchases—see a clear category-wise breakdown.

Actionable Tips

Personalized suggestions appear with your result to reduce the biggest sources first.

Benchmarks

Compare your weekly footprint with the recommended range aligned to climate targets.

History (Local)

Save results locally in your browser and track progress over time—no login required.

Privacy First

All calculations run in your browser. No data leaves your device unless you opt-in for AI advice.

How to Use (Step-by-Step)

  1. Open Calculate and enter your values (choose weekly/monthly/yearly).
  2. Click Calculate to see your total and a category-wise breakdown + chart.
  3. Review tips shown below the result to cut your largest sources.
  4. Press Save Result to store it locally and monitor your progress.
  5. Click Download Report (PDF) for a printable summary.
  6. Optionally, press Get Smart Advice (AI) to receive LLM‑generated recommendations.

Data Assumptions (Defaults)

Transportation

Average: 150 km per week
Emission Factor: 0.21 kg CO₂ / km

Energy Usage

Average: 75 kWh per week (≈ 300 kWh / month)
Emission Factor: 0.257 kg CO₂ / kWh

Food Consumption

Average: 21 meals per week
Emission Factor: 1.2 kg CO₂ / meal

Waste Production

Average: 10 kg per week
Emission Factor: 0.9 kg CO₂ / kg

Purchasing Habits

Average: 10 items per month (≈ 2.5 per week)
Emission Factor: 2.5 kg CO₂ / item
